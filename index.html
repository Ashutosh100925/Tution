Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enroll my child?

Enrollment begins with filling out the form below. After submission, our academic counselor will contact you to schedule a brief diagnostic assessment for your child. Based on the results, we will recommend the most suitable batch.

What are the batch timings?

We offer both weekday evening and weekend batches to accommodate school schedules. Specific timings are alloted post-admission to ensure homogenous peer groups.

What is the fee structure?

Our fees reflect the premium, personalized nature of our program. Prices range from $900 to $2,500 annually depending on the chosen curriculum. We offer flexible quarterly installment plans.

Are classes available online or offline?

We provide a hybrid model. Core conceptual classes are conducted in-person at our state-of-the-art center, while doubt clearing and revision sessions are accessible online via our premium portal.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes, shortlisted candidates can attend one masterclass before confirming enrollment to experience our unique pedagogical framework firsthand.
