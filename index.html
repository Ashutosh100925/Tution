Frequently Asked Questions

Do you teach both CBSE and ICSE students?

Yes, we have specialized batches for both CBSE and ICSE/ISC boards. Each batch follows a curriculum strictly aligned with their respective board's latest syllabus and examination patterns.

Are classes available online or offline?

We primarily offer in-person classes at our dedicated chemistry center for maximum interaction. However, we do provide live online support and recorded revision masterclasses for all enrolled students.

Are detailed study materials provided?

Absolutely. Every student receives curated chapter-wise notes, daily practice sheets (DPPs), and board-specialized mock test papers designed personally by Dr. Soubhagya Dash.

Is there personal doubt-clearing support?

Personalized mentorship is our core value. We have dedicated doubt sessions every weekend, and students can also raise conceptual queries via our private student portal at any time.

How can I enroll for the 2026 Batch?

You can start by filling out the enrollment inquiry form below. Our team will schedule a counseling call followed by a quick diagnostic test to assess the student's current conceptual standing.
